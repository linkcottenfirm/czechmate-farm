Our Family Story

The name "Czechmate" is more than clever wordplay — it's a tribute to our Czech heritage and the pride we take in everything we do.

Located in Lillington, NC, we're a family-owned farm supply providing quality hay, shavings, and grain to horse owners, farmers, and animal lovers throughout Central North Carolina.

Whether you need a few bales for your backyard horses or regular deliveries for your stable, we're here to help. Stop by the farm or give us a call!
